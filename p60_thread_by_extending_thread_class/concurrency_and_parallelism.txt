Concurrency-
          Performing a single task at a time but managing multiple task at a same,
          know to be a concurrency. 

    example:
           Suppose a student study and play cricket in day ,
           when he study he do not play cricket , when he play cricket he do not study.

Parallelism:
            Performing mutiple task at a time known to be Parallelism.
        example:
            Suppose a person typed by one hand write by another hand at a same time .
